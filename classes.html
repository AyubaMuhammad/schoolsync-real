Our Classes

Discover our diverse range of educational programs designed to nurture young minds

We bring you the essentials of everyday life through engaging, hands-on learning. In this class, children explore topics like health care basics, community roles, and practical tools all designed to build awareness and curiosity. From understanding doctor visits to discovering useful books and resources, Ciclos helps young minds connect with the world around them. We also introduce elements of nature and travel, with fun references to parks, supplies, and places like Bayern sparking imagination and global thinking.
